History of Sarasota

  1. First appeared on a sheepskin map in 1763 as Zarazota.
  2. Fort Armistead was established in 1840.
  3. The Florida Morgage and Investment Company of Edinburgh bought 60k acres that would become Sarasota.
  4. Sarasota incorperated as a town in 1902.

  5. Civil Rights in Sarasota

    • protests started october second 1955 over segregated beaches
    • In 1956 a city ordinance passed that allowed police to clear a beach if more than one race was present
    • Town built a pool instead, but protests started again after it was completed.
    • NAACP sues for not integrating their schools.
    • In 1962 they integated their first school, and began busing students to previously all-white schools.
    • In 1967 the city passes another ordinance in support of segregated beaches sparking protests again.
  6. 2000-2008 saw massive real estate bubble then crash, believed to have started in Sarasota
