History of Beirut

  1. Groups Before Ottoman Rule:

    • Phonecian
    • Mamluk
    • Hellenistic
    • Roman
    • Macedonia
    • Byzantine
  2. Ottoman Empire conquered then Syria, which includes present day Lebanon, in the early 1500s.
  3. During WWI, suffered the Great Famine of Mount Lebanon.
  4. After the fall of the Ottoman Empire, the League of Nations mandated the area go to France.
  5. Lebanon gains intdependence in 1943 and names Beirut its capital.
  6. Lebanese Civil War from 1975-1990.
  7. In 2020 the port explodes killing hundreds and injuring thousands.
